International Affairs Cell

Gujarat Forensic Sciences University is one of the very few Universities in the Country, to establish a full-fledged Cell for Coordination of International Affairs, to look into matters concerning overseas tie-ups in terms of joint academic programs; collaborations; events; trainings; summer internships; overseas scholarship programs (for home students only) and so on.

Overseas Applicants are more than Welcome to apply for various courses at GFSU.

International students are given admissions based upon Merit only. They Do Not require appearing in University’s Entrance Examinations meant for Home Students or submit Official Scores for any of the National/State level Entrance Tests (which is a requirement for Home students, seeking admission into some of our courses). Applicants may visit Institutional Pages on this website for more information on Academic Programs; General Eligibility Criteria and Fee Structure (meant for Foreign Students); etc.

Admissions OPEN in May every year. Keep checking Notice Board on website for updates.

Additionally, all International Students are required to submit Proof for English Language Qualification (in form of any internationally acclaimed Certifications/Scores, as  – TOEFL/IELTS/Cambridge ESOL Certifications/Others), in case their first language is not English and/or  they do not have an already acquired educational qualification, pursued using English as the medium of study.

International Students may also have to go through certain necessary procedures required for Security / Background Check, before being admitted to this University.

Kindly Note: It is mandatory for all International Students, to submit copies of their Passport and Visa, along with other required documents (check application procedure for your chosen course, for list of documents), when applying to this University.

Depending on the Country of your Residence, students may also have to produce a Letter of Clearance, issued by their respective Country’s Consulate in India and/or their Parent Organization in their Home Country (in case of working applicants), before joining the University.

This University also undertakes specially tailored Short Term Training Programs / Educational Courses for large group of Working Officials / Students from outside India, upon prior request.

Three International Students have been sponsored by Indian Council for Cultural Relations (ICCR) at GFSU.
